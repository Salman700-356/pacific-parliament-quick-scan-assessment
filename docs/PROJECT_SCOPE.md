# Pacific Parliament Quick Scan Assessment (PPQSA)

## What this is
A lightweight cyber maturity quick scan for Pacific Parliaments.

## Core goals
- Easy to complete (10–20 minutes)
- Consistent scoring across parliaments
- Secure storage of answers
- Regional benchmarking (admin view)
- Repeat assessments over time (trend reporting)

## Assessment design
- 8 pillars
- 30 questions
- Score per question: 0–3 (plus optional N/A)
- Total score out of 24 (sum of pillar averages)

## Roles
### Participant (Parliament user)
- Starts via invite link
- Completes the questionnaire
- Submits and views results

### Admin (Programme owner)
- Creates invite links
- Views maturity across all parliaments
- Sees common struggle areas
- Exports results
